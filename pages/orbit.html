Mission Lesson

Orbit

Orbit is controlled falling. A rocket moves sideways fast enough that Earth curves away beneath it.

Nexus I Goal

Reach A Stable Path

  • Apoapsis The highest point of the flight path. Nexus I raises this first.
  • Periapsis The lowest point of the flight path. A stable orbit needs this above the atmosphere.
  • Circularization A sideways burn near apoapsis that turns a high arc into a true orbit.

SFS Note

Turn Slowly

Start nearly vertical, then lean gradually. A smooth turn saves fuel and keeps Nexus I easier to control.
