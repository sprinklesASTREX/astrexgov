Space

Space is the next design problem after orbit: distance, navigation, fuel, timing, and survival.

Astrex Path

After Nexus I

  • Satellites First payloads prove that the rocket can put useful objects into orbit.
  • Stations Later Nexus builds can dock modules and create a base above Earth.
  • Moon And Mars Deep-space flights need larger boosters, transfer windows, and careful staging.
  • Nexus IV Target The long-range plan is to orbit the Moon and bring the spacecraft home cleanly.
  • Mission Data Every Nexus flight adds information about fuel margin, orbital control, staging, and return planning.

Long-Term Target

Build Toward Mars

Nexus I starts small on purpose. Every clean launch teaches the next rocket how to carry more, travel farther, and waste less.
