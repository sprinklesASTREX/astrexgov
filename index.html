Real Program Plan

Nexus I-IV

Nexus I

Crewed mission deploying DOJ military satellites, plus test runs for orbiting with thin rockets.

Nexus II

Sends a satellite to orbit and keeps it around Earth for 16 days to see how the rocket system lasts through two weeks.

Nexus III

Simulation-focused tests for failures, abort decisions, and landing protocol practice.

Nexus IV

Orbits the Moon and returns home.
